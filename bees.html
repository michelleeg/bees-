Rules of the Game

Hi! My name is Ernesto! I love flowers! Can you help me pollinate as many flowers as I can?

This is how you play:
- use the mouse to move Ernesto
- click on the flowers to pollinate them
- when you pollinate all six flowers, go to the beehive to drop off the pollen and collect 50 bonus points. Then go back out to pollinate more flowers!
- watch out for clouds of pesticides! If Ernesto runs into a cloud, he will die and the game will be over
